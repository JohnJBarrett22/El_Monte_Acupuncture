About

Dr. Jay J. Ryu, L.Ac, Ph.D. is a licensed acupuncturist and herbalist. He holds a Ph.D. from the prestigious Yuin University and earned a Masters degree in Traditional Oriental Medicine from the renowned Dongguk Royal University where he was a professor in Oriental Medicine. Dr. Ryu has been in private practice since 1997. Dr. Ryu serves as a core member of the Hongik Oriental Medical Association with extensive research in Oriental Medicine and Herbology. He has also been a continuing education instructor for acupuncturists. Dr. Ryu specializes in infertility, allergies, asthma, and pain management for conditions such as back pain and neck pain. Dr. Ryu

Ryu Acupuncture Clinic first opened in 1997 in Burbank, California.

Dr. Jay J. Ryu, L.Ac, Ph.D. is a licensed acupuncturist and herbalist.  He holds a Ph.D. from the prestigious Yuin University and earned a Masters degree in Traditional Oriental Medicine from the renowned Dongguk Royal University where he was a professor in Oriental Medicine.

He serves as a core member of the Hongik Oriental Medical Association with extensive research in Oriental Medicine and Herbology.  Dr. Ryu has also been a continuing education instructor for acupuncturists.

He is fluent in both English and Korean.  Dr. Ryu has glowing success in issues regarding infertility, allergies, asthma, facial rejuvenation, pain management, and female issues.
