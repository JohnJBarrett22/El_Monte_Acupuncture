OUR SERVICES

Services to balance your life

We offer a wide range of holistic preventative and rehabilitative services. Eastern Medicine has been used to treat and prevent disease for over 2,500 years across the Asian continent and has been proven effective for a wide array of chronic conditions. Fortunately, it is becoming more mainstream in the West. During your initial visit, we will work together to determine the cause of your condition through an evaluation of symptoms, behaviors and lifestyle. Additional examination such as history, look, feel, taste, smell and your ultimately your pulse will help guide the course of your treatment.

Acupuncture

Acupuncture is the insertion of very thin needles through your skin at specific points of the body. A staple of traditional Chinese medicine, acupuncture is most commonly used to treat pain and stress.

Traditional Chinese Herbs

Chinese herbal medicine uses herbs to improve overall health, while combatting a wide-variety of conditions and ailments. This approach helps to balance the body by treating patient patterns.

Cupping

Cupping is a form of alternative therapy originating in China. It involves placing cups on the skin to form suction that may help facilitate healing with the increased blood flow to the area of treatment.

Auriculotherapy

Ear acupuncture, or auriculotherapy, is a form of medicine based on the notion that the ear is a micro system that reflects the body. The auricle, or outer portion of the ear, is where treatment is performed.

Moxibustion

A traditional Chinese medicine, Moxibustion is a therapy that consists of burning dried mugwort on particular points of the body. This is often used as a treatment for a wide-range variety of conditions.

Heat Therapy

Also called thermotherapy, heat therapy is the use of heat to treat pain and improve overall health. This is often beneficial to patients with ailments such as arthritis, stiff muscles or deep tissue injuries.
